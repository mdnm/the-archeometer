<h3>The Cosmological Archeometer</h3>

<h4 class="italic">Revealer and Regulator of Higher Studies</h4>

<h4>Detailed Description</h4>

<h5>The Archeometer and Architechny</h5>

<p>The Archeometer is thus the precision instrument of higher sciences and their corresponding arts, their cosmometric protractor, their cosmological standard, their regulator and homological revealer.</p>

<p>It brings them back to their single universal principle, to their mutual concordance and to their synarchic synthesis.</p>

<p>This synthesis, which is none other than the genesis of the principle, is the very Word, and it autographs its own name upon the first triangle of the Archeometer: <i>SOPh-Ya</i>, Wisdom of God.</p>

<p>But to make understood all possible applications of the Archeometer as experimental revealer and regulator of this genesis and synthesis would require entering into endless developments.</p>

<p>It would require inventorying the entire nomenclature of the quadruple hierarchy of substances, facts, and consequently of divine, angelic, human and natural sciences and arts.</p>

<p>Moreover, it would require indicating, while entering into the universality of consequences, all equivalences and all correspondences of these hierarchies.</p>

<p>We shall reach the same demonstrative goal by a swifter path&mdash;that of fact alone, of experience in all its scientific truth and consequently in all the conscientious integrity one must demand of oneself in so grave a matter.</p>

<p>This fact is art&mdash;but art considered as the creative and conscious utterance of science itself, no longer as the individualist manifestation of anarchy, fancy, fashion or imitation.</p>

<p>This is why we concentrate the Archeometer's applications upon that Art which may become the most direct expression of Science returned to its principle&mdash;upon the art that synthesizes all sciences in action, all arts, all crafts and industries&mdash;in a word, the entire hierarchy of human labor.</p>

<p>This art is Architechny.</p>

<p>The masterwork of Architechny being the Religious Edifice&mdash;summary of the Principle of Law and of social fact&mdash;we shall apply the Archeometer to the science of religions.</p>

<p>Being potentially required to erect Christian cathedrals, Brahmanic pagodas<sup><a href="#T-31" id="ref-T-31">[&#42;]</a></sup>, Buddhist or Chinese temples, Zoroastrian fire temples or Muslim mosques, the architect will see upon the Archeometric Regulator the exact position of each of these religions within Religion&mdash;this word being used in its sense of scientific Synthesis and of Wisdom in the ancient meaning of the term.</p>

<p>The Archeometer will experimentally prove itself to be both Revealer and Regulator of Higher Studies&mdash;the Revealer of the Revelation foretold at the beginning of the 19<sup>th</sup> century by the Count de Maistre.</p>

<p>The Archeometric Principle being the Utterance, the architect will first use the double zone of letters, and this will simultaneously provide him with all its equivalences in sonometric numbers, colors, notes, musical modes, and consequently morphological forms.</p>

<p>He will only need to pass from the World of Glory to that of the astral Heavens to obtain the cosmological correspondences resulting from the preceding angelic and divine ones.</p>

<h5>Crown of 360 degrees or 36 decans</h5>

<p>The number 36, in decimal Sanskrit letters, is written: GO.</p>

<p>In the Veda, it signifies Heaven itself. The number 360, in Adamic numerical letters, is written: <i>ShaS</i>. It signifies the sexennial power 6&nbsp;&times;&nbsp;6&mdash;that which precisely corresponds to the measurement of the circle by the hexagon.</p>

<p>We shall later see the importance of this relation to the very Principle. The latter has, as its characteristic, the Trinity determining its direct instrumentality in sexenniality: 3...6; the equilateral trigone determining the hexagonal star.</p>

<p>This double circular protractor with inverse progression, regarding degrees, has a function of considerable practical verification&mdash;as facts will later attest&mdash;particularly concerning colors, the luminous Equivalents of the Utterance: Rays and colors.</p>

<p>Let us recall here, for the record, that the Gospel of Saint John, read in Aramaic Syriac, states: "The Principle is the Utterance, the Word." In patriarchal Greece&mdash;or rather in Slavic Balkans&mdash;Orpheus, according to traditions collected by the Church Fathers, had left among his numerous canonical books a work entitled: <i>The Utterance or the Sacred Word</i>. Likewise in patriarchal Italy, that of the Etruscans.</p>

<p>One need only explore antiquity somewhat across all parts of the world to find certain traces of the importance of human Utterance, considered as a reflection of the divine Word.</p>

<p>Undoubtedly, from India to China, from Slavdom and Scandinavia to ancient America, from Syria and Chaldea to Egypt, erudition can only reach the superstitious and magical remnants of the ancient science of this primordial Utterance and its alphabets.</p>

<p>Yet these very relics bear witness to this lost science.</p>

<h4>The Alphabets</h4>

<p>Among ancient alphabets predating the anarchistic Greco-Latin civilizations, we classify those with 22 numeral letters as typical equivalents of the Utterance.</p>

<p>We name them Solar and Solar-Lunar, with the understanding that these astral names are merely signs of correspondence between the World of Glory and the astral World.</p>

<p>It is through forgetting this distinction&mdash;taking effect for cause, consequence for Principle&mdash;that certain ancient Universities fell into the worship of astral Powers, Angels and demons; into Sabaeanism, even into fetishism; and that, a century ago, Dupuis plunged lower still into the crudest astronomical materialism.</p>

<p>We classify as Lunar the alphabets of 28 letters, as Zodiacal Horary those of 24, as Zodiacal Monthly those of 30, as Decanic those of 36, etc.&mdash;always under the aforementioned reservations, and relating all these numbers to XXII as the Standard.</p>

<p>The alphabet of the first Patriarchs is that which we employ upon the Archeometer, for the following reasons.</p>

<p>It is morphological&mdash;that is, more than geometric; and through its rigid or flexible forms at will, it depicts the object it names, or names its form, according to rules unnecessary to expound here.</p>

<p>The Zodiacal and Planetary Signs derive from it, as does the construction of the sphere or planisphere containing these signs.</p>

<p>Consequently, the cosmological function and position of each letter are determined by its formal kinship with the astral signs, whose placement is itself determined astronomically.</p>

<p>It follows that since letters position themselves thus and not by human hand, their placement, their binary and ternary groupings, all their interrelations are autological rather than anthropological. We join to these, upon the Archeometer, the Syrian, Assyrian (so-called Hebrew), Samaritan and Chaldean alphabets&mdash;all Solar or Solar-Lunar, with XXII literal and numerical equivalents.</p>

<p>So that when the resemblance between the Archeometric letter and its corresponding Zodiacal sign leaves some visual uncertainty, this is dispelled by the analogous letter in other alphabets&mdash;principally the Samaritan.</p>

<h5>Lunar alphabet: Vedic signs derived from the point of AUM</h5>

<p>After having deeply studied for several years the oral teachings of their most learned pontiffs, we have therefore also rejected their transposition from Y to M, basing this on our personal study of their mysteries and on very precise indications contained in the Gospels and Epistles.</p>

<p id="note-1a" class="text-sm text-gray-500 dark:text-gray-400 mt-4">(1) See <i>La Th&eacute;ogonie des Patriarches</i> by Saint-Yves. 1 handsome vol. large quarto. <a href="#ref-note-1a" class="text-indigo-600 dark:text-indigo-400">&#8617;</a></p>

<p><i>Construction of the Archeometer as a double semi-circular protractor, with all equivalents of the Utterance, the Sanskrit letters corresponding to the Adamic.</i></p>

<p>Here is how we have employed the XXII letters in constructing the Archeometer. Of these XXII, III give the centers of each semicircle, the diameter, and the circumference presented as a double semicircle.</p>

<p>The Gospel contains this key: "I am the Aleph and the Thau," which has been translated into Greek as: "The Alpha and the Omega."</p>

<p>This translation shifts the mystery from the real to the mystical, the Greek language being a Sudra, Prakrit or mongrel tongue, not archeometric.</p>

<p>In Assyrian script called Hebrew, the A consists of a crossbar and two points &#183;/&#183;.</p>

<p>In Adamic morphological script, the bar indicates the radius or diameter, and alone constitutes the letter A; in the same script, the two points indicate a doubled center and the letter S; the Th indicates a doubled circumference in two inverted semicircles thus: &#8484;</p>

<p>This is why, considering the <i>Aleph</i> as a doubled diameter, its two points as centers, the <i>Thau</i> as a double semicircle, we have assigned these three morphological letters to the Construction of the figure that bears the name 'Zodiac of the Utterance' as a double protractor.</p>

<p>Here is this form: &#8484; <sup><a href="#note-1b" id="ref-note-1b">(1)</a></sup>.</p>

<p>It is the Brazen Serpent of Moses, to which allusion is also made in the Gospel. It is the Orphic Caduceus.</p>

<p>These three Adamic letters A, S, Th, these two Assyrian letters A, Th, thus signify the Triple Divine Power constituting the Universal Type; the Circle signifies the Infinite; the Center, the Absolute; the Radius or diameter their manifestation, their interrelation.</p>

<p>Thus, of XXII letters, III pertain to the constitutive Power. The remaining XIX refer to the distributive Powers of universal harmonicity and organicity.</p>

<p>Of these XIX, XII are involutive, VII are evolutive, in the World of Glory or of the Word, and consequently in that of the astral Heavens.</p>

<p>In other words, XII letters are zodiacal, VII are planetary&mdash;or rather VI planetary evolving around a solar one&mdash;which Jews and Greeks were unaware of.</p>
