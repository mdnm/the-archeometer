The Name reappears in that Prophet's prediction of the Savior: Sous-IOSh. Finally in Moses — heir of the Patriarchs — ShWa-DHA becomes ShADAI, literally "Self-Giving God," and since this word lacks Hebrew roots, we reiterate that Kabbalists and Talmudic Rabbis have debated it since Simeon ben Yochai.</p>

<p>These correspondences — which cannot be accidental — are proofs of the Proto-Synthesis and its action through Deutosyntheses beginning in Krishna's era, continuing via the Abrahamids, Moses, Orpheus and Pythagoras unbroken until the Incarnation of the Redeeming Word — Our Lord Jesus Christ.</p>

<p>"And the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us; and He gave to all — to all who believe in His Name, in His SheMa — the power to become Sons of God," within a Humanity restored to the Image of the World of Glory.</p>

<p>Such is the Information, such is the perspective of Higher Studies that Christianity ensures, providing evidence of the True and restoring all things to their real and non-illusory perspective.</p>

<p>Such may be the State of Man reintegrated into divine Biology. It is the complete theological State, already super-living in the enharmonic or trinitarian Unity of eternal Life-God, in His living Mathesis and all vital potentialities unknown to those ignorant of the powers and possibilities inherent in this triple State.</p>

<p>All is vivified therein, and this is why the Intellectuality and Utterance of theological Books — above all the New Testament — remain seven times sealed to the stillborn Intellect of philosophers, subjective metaphysicians and dialecticians, and to the born-dead Intellect of minds regressed into their external physical and physiological Senses.</p>

<p>All senses have their degree of latent or existing Life; the external senses possess the least: the anthropoid Animal of the Self; the internal senses possess the animated human: the sociable Individual's Androic; the intimate senses possess the animating principle, the objectively impersonalized person: the Androtheste in Our Lord Jesus Christ, the Social aspect of the Biology of the Double Universe, whose center is Life itself.</p>

<p>None has ever fully possessed this ultimate sense save God alone in His Word — the Essence in its Principle of Existence or divine Humanity. Hence the quality signified by this word is the divine Species of androic men in God, conjoining within them the human person to the second divine Person: the vivified Image of the vivifying Image of the Living-God: Jesus.</p>
