<p>The Thousand-Year Cycles are chromatic and are themselves measured into similar periods or Octaves of Five hundred years. Their harmony or triplicity unfolds through three millennia divided into six-hundred-year periods.</p>

<p>Thus from Pythagoras to Hierocles spans one millennium during which Mediterranean Paganism lived—dragging into its demise, after having annihilated them, most ethnic divisions of the ancient patriarchal Empire, itself in decline a millennium before Pythagoras.</p>

<p>This millennium divides into two five-hundred-year periods. From Pythagoras to Julius Caesar—five hundred years—Nimrod's apotheosis is renewed. All ancient Oriental Paganism is completely reflected and aggravated in the West. It is then that the Word worshipped by the Patriarchs incarnates and, within Himself, above all Humanity, restores His entire Tradition, His entire Revelation past and future.</p>

<p>Five centuries later, continuing His work from the Throne of the Invisible, He strips the Caesars of Apotheosis, rendering unto God what is God's: the Principle, the Law, the teaching Reason and social Reason of Humanity. Henceforth, the Caesars' heads are bowed by Him under the spiritual power of the Apostles represented by the Resurrection of a universal Patriarch and as many Patriarchs as ethnic Churches.</p>

<p>Then appears Hierocles. Five centuries after him, all ethnicities annihilated by pagan Rome are resurrected under the blessing of Jesus Christ's Patriarchs, and their vivification advances toward the fulfillment of His civilization, His social State, His Promise of God's reign on Earth as in Heaven. France at their head, Nations revive in the Gospel's Breath.</p>

<p>Five centuries later, the Anti-Word, the great Adversary, makes the pagan Spirit emerge from its Hell: the pagan humanist Renaissance.</p>

<p>Five centuries more, and Europe's social Unity is so utterly annihilated that this entire Continent now lies at the mercy of Asia and America.</p>
